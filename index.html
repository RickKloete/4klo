The 8-Step Process

A proprietary system,
not a sales pitch.

Built around the three things hiring leaders actually care about: quality candidates, faster delivery, and real accountability. This is how we close searches other firms walk away from.

STEP 01

Discovery & Role Design

Deep-dive intake with the hiring leader. We define the seat, the success metrics, and the cultural shape of the right hire.

STEP 02

Compensation Benchmarking

Market-grade comp analysis so the offer holds up. No guessing, no losing finalists to a number you could have anticipated.

STEP 03

Sourcing Strategy

Target maps built off proprietary networks, AI-informed talent pools, and 20+ years of executive relationships across industries.

STEP 04

Direct Outreach

We approach the executives who are actually doing the work — not the ones who are job-hunting. Quiet, professional, persistent.

STEP 05

Vetting & Assessment

Multi-layer evaluation against role criteria, leadership capability, and cultural fit. Candidates earn their way to your inbox.

STEP 06

Finalist Presentation

You receive multiple hireable finalists for the same seat. Clients regularly tell us the hardest part was choosing.

STEP 07

Offer & Close

We quarterback the close — counter-offers, references, comp negotiation — so the finalist signs and starts on time.

STEP 08

1-Year Guarantee

If the placement doesn't work in the first year, we re-run the search. Most firms won't touch this. We require it of ourselves.

Frequently Asked

The questions hiring
leaders actually ask first.

What kinds of roles does Rick Kloete recruit for?
Mid-to-senior leadership roles across virtually every industry — VP, SVP, C-suite, and direct reports — with a particular depth in manufacturing, healthcare, financial services, higher education, supply chain, and technology. The niche isn't industry; it's leadership level.
What is the 1-year placement guarantee?
If a placement we make doesn't work out in the first 12 months, we re-run the search at no additional search fee. It's one of the strongest guarantees in the executive search industry, and most firms won't touch it. We require it of ourselves because real accountability is what separates a search partner from a vendor.
How fast can you deliver finalist candidates?
Our 8-step process consistently delivers finalists in roughly one-third less time than the market average — often with multiple hireable candidates for the same seat. Speed without sacrificing fit is the entire point of the process.
How do your fees compare to other executive search firms?
Our retained search fees run approximately 25% below industry averages. You're not paying less for less work — you're paying for a boutique partner without the big-firm overhead, with a guarantee most large firms won't offer.
Do you only work with companies in Tennessee?
No. Kloete Group is headquartered in Franklin, Tennessee, but we run retained searches for clients across the United States and have placed leaders into global operations for multinational clients.
Can Rick help with executive compensation, even without a search?
Yes. Rick provides independent executive compensation benchmarking and offer-architecture work for HR leaders at Fortune 100 firms as well as for executives in transition. It's a separate engagement from search and can be scoped as a project or retainer.
How do I start a conversation?
The fastest path is the 30-minute Zoom link: calendly.com/rkloete/30-minute-zoom-call. You can also call (615) 823-7723 or email rick@4klo.com.
